Our Products

Llama Inc has some of the finest organic vegan llama clothing in the world. Our assortment includes everything from socks, sweaters, scarfs to hats. You name it, and we got it! We use only the most premium wool for our products to make sure that the customer is satisfied. Llama Inc strives to become as eco-friendly as humanly possible, and that’s why we do everything from washing the wool to packing up the products in-house in our own eco-friendly factory. By doing this we are able to reduce travel time when making our products, aswell as making sure every step of the way is done with absolute precision.

The classic beanies

On this page you will see examples of some our most popular products. This Llama Original Beanie for instance, is a timeless classic that never will go out of style. Hand knit in our own factory the beanie is made of 100% organic Llama wool. It will keep you warm and stylish at the same time! The beanie is available in three different colors: grey, brown and beige. You can also get it in three different sizes: small, medium and large.

Baby llama wool scarfs

Our scarfs are a perfect match for the classic Llama beanies. Knit in our factory. What's special about these scarfs however is that they're made of 100% baby llama wool. They are available in grey, brown and beige. Have you ever felt the softness of cashmere? That is what the baby llama wool feels like. It will keep you protected against drafts and chills, and it will feel extremely comfortable at the same time. Even though we call it baby llama wool it doesn't necessarily mean that it actually came from a baby llama. Even older llamas are able to produce fiber that falls into the "baby" category. In other words it's just a classification used to describe the group and micron count of the fibers taken from the animal.

Llama wool designer socks

Designer socks made with 100% organic llama wool. Also hand knit in our factory. They come in three different color styles. Super comfortable to wear and will keep your feet ward at all times. We have sizes for everyone including children. Good breathing capabilites so you can wear them on long trips aswell. We also do custom socks for those that are interested.
