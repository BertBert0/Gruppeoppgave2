The Llama-Barn is both the headquarters of Llama Inc and where the magic happens, and with magic I mean turning that fluffy llama wool into the soft and warm orgasmic pieces of clothing you (we) all know and love. This state of the art facility is equipped with not only shears of unimaginable quality but it also has an espresso machine. Visitors can also try their hand at dung-heaving, but do be wary of the Llamas, they don't bite, but they spit like hell.

To reach The Llama-Barn first you obviously got to step off the boat. Follow the road to the north-east until you reach the log and take a right, if you brought mountain-climbing gear ignore the map and just keep going north-east. When you've passed the burnt-down hut adjust course 8 degrees due east and keep going. If you did not bring mountain-climbing gear keep following the road that is now taking you due south until you see the lighthouse, then take the first left. Then simply follow the road past the orchard. At the top of the hill take a left and you're there!
