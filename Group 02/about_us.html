Llama inc and its history


Llama Inc was established in 1870 by the worlds most renowned llama expert John L Lamar. He started out in a remote mountain village in Peru where he quickly became famous for his exquisite tailoring skills and later created the infamous Lamar sewing technique ® which is used to this day.

By the turn of the twentieth century he owned and operated 36 clothing stores all over the Americas, and people from all walks of life could be seen wearing llama scarfs, hats and socks. He retired in 1939 at the ripe old age of 69 and gave the company over to his two sons, Travis and Chadrick Lamar. The brothers supplied over one million socks to the US army in the second world war, leading to a nationwide shortage of llama wool for a long period.


After several bad investments in the late 80's and early 90's the brothers were forced to sell or shut down most of their clothing stores, except the one in Norway. Today Llama Inc is the number one supplier of llamas and llama related products in Bø - Telemark (All our other locations are currently closed)
